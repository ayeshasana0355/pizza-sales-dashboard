## ✨ Key Features

- 📐 Custom DAX Measures — revenue, averages, order counts & trend calculations
- 🎛 Dynamic Slicers — filter by Pizza Category and Date Range
- 🔗 Cross-filter Interactions — all visuals respond to each other
- 📅 Time Intelligence — daily, monthly, and quarterly trend analysis
- 🏆 Best/Worst Seller Analysis — ranked by both revenue and quantity
- 🔄 Power Query Transformations — clean, reliable data pipeline
- 🎨 Consistent Branding — purple and gold color palette throughout

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📅 **Busiest Days:** Fridays and Saturdays have the highest order volumes
- 📆 **Busiest Months:** July and January peak in total orders
- 🏅 **Top Category:** Classic pizzas lead in both sales and total orders
- 📏 **Top Size:** Large size pizzas contribute the most to overall revenue
- 🍗 **Top Pizza (Revenue):** Thai Chicken & Barbecue Chicken (~$43K each)
- 🍕 **Top Pizza (Quantity):** Classic Deluxe & Barbecue Chicken (2.5K each)
- 📈 **Weekday Dominance:** Weekdays drive **72.81%** of total revenue
- 📊 **Peak Quarter:** Q2 leads (~$205K); Q4 is the lowest (~$170K)
